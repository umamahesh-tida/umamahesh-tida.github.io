Research & Funded Projects

Advancing the frontiers of IC Design Automation, Edge-AI Hardware, and Biomedical Systems

Research Areas

Our Core Research Focus

Six interconnected areas driving innovation in hardware and design automation.

🔲

Monolithic 3D IC (M3D-IC) Design Automation

EDA frameworks for M3D-IC: partitioning algorithms, placement with bottom-gate transistors, MIV shielding strategies, and heterogeneous substrate integration. Our NSF CAREER-funded work targets the next generation of 3D chip fabrication.

🤖

Edge-AI Hardware Accelerators

Custom VLSI accelerators for machine learning inference at the edge — targeting ultra-low-power embedded systems, wearables, and biomedical implants that cannot rely on cloud connectivity.

⚡

Power Delivery Networks & Magnetics

On-chip power delivery using magnetic-core solenoid inductors and TSV-inductor-based multi-phase buck converters. Addressing voltage integrity challenges in 3D stacked systems.

🧬

Sensor Fusion & Biomedical Systems

RF electric field and machine learning-based biosensors for early cancer detection, agriculture, and point-of-care diagnostics. Integration of dielectrophoresis and elliptical dichroism spectrometry with ML pipelines.

🧠

Neuromorphic & In-Memory Computing

Exploring MIV-transistor-based SRAM for M3D integration and energy-efficient architectures inspired by neuromorphic computing for next-generation AI hardware.

📡

RFID & Agricultural Sensing

NSF- and USDA-funded research on RFID sensing systems with embedded ML for real-time local decisions in agriculture — herbicide-resistant weed identification and precision farming.
