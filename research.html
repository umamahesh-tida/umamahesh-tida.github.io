Our Research Focus

The BISON CAD Lab drives cutting-edge research in:

  • Monolithic 3D Integrated Circuits (M3D-IC) Design Automation
  • EDA Frameworks for IC Design and Manufacturing
  • Edge-AI Hardware Accelerators for Embedded Systems
  • Sensor Fusion and Bio-sensor Integration Systems
  • Power Delivery Networks with Magnetic-core Inductors
  • Neuromorphic Computing for Energy-efficient AI

Featured Project Roadmap

This roadmap shows our Monolithic 3D CAD Automation objectives, including partitioning, placement with bottom-gate transistors, MIV shielding, and heterogeneous integration strategies. Our work also explores advanced power delivery solutions and design for ML hardware.
