Lab News & Announcements

Awards, grants, student achievements, and milestones from the BISON CAD Lab.

Grants & Research Highlights

4
June 2025

🎓 Four Students Participated in Young Fellows Program at Design Automation Conference

Congratulations to Yuma Ishimura, Ifedayo Akinfemisoye, Navya Goli, and Tejaswi Dhandu for being selected to participate in the prestigious Young Fellows Program at the Design Automation Conference (DAC) 2025! This program recognizes outstanding graduate students in EDA, chip design, and related fields.

June 2025

🌐 NSF IRES Award for International Research Experience

Dr. Tida received an NSF IRES grant as Co-PI, supporting international research experiences on RFID Sensing Systems with Machine Learning. This grant provides students with the opportunity to engage in cutting-edge international research collaborations in advanced CAD methodologies and hardware.

March 2025

🔬 NSF Award for Future Manufacturing – CAR T-Cell Therapy

Dr. Tida received an NSF Future Manufacturing grant as Co-PI. The award supports research into novel, low-cost, viral-free manufacturing strategies for CAR T-cell therapies, developing distributed, mRNA-based methods to engineer Chimeric Antigen Receptor T-cells without viral gene transfer — aiming to reduce costs and improve safety for advanced cell-based cancer treatments.

February 2025

🏆 NSF CAREER Award Received by Dr. Tida

We are proud to announce that Dr. Umamaheswara Rao Tida has received the prestigious NSF CAREER Award — $553,560 over 5 years (2025–2030) — to support research on advanced process technology-based EDA frameworks for efficient Monolithic 3D Integration. This award was received on the very first attempt, reflecting the transformative potential and rigor of the proposed research.
