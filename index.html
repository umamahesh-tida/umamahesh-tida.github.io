Welcome

Welcome to the personal website of Dr. Umamaheswara Rao Tida, Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at North Dakota State University. His research interests span Monolithic 3D Integrated Circuits (M3D-IC), VLSI CAD Tools, Edge-AI accelerators, neuromorphic computing, and sensor fusion for bioelectronics.
