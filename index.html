Dr. Umamaheswara Rao Tida

Director, ACE² Lab · Assistant Professor of ECE
ACE2 — AI, Circuits & EDA · Automated Circuit Engineering

Dr. Tida leads the ACE² Lab at North Dakota State University, conducting research in IC design automation, monolithic 3D IC EDA, ML-driven ASIC design, and ASIC accelerators for LLM inference. His research is supported by NSF CAREER, NSF CRII, USDA, and other agencies, totaling over $6.97M in competitive grants.

Prospective Students

We are actively recruiting motivated Ph.D. and M.S. students.

How to Apply: We are actively looking for motivated Ph.D. and M.S. students with a strong background in electrical or computer engineering, computer science, or related fields. Research areas include IC design automation, hardware for machine learning, monolithic 3D IC EDA, and power delivery networks.

Please send the following to umamaheswara.tida@ieee.org:
01
Curriculum Vitae (CV) — including your academic background, research experience, and publications if any.
02
Unofficial Transcripts — from all undergraduate and graduate institutions attended.
03
Statement of Interest — a brief paragraph describing your research interests and why you want to join ACE² Lab.
04
Research Area of Interest — M3D-IC EDA, ML for ASIC Design, ASIC for LLM Acceleration, or Power Delivery.
05
GRE Scores — Quantitative score > 165 is required.
