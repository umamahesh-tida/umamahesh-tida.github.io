Research Focus

Cutting-Edge Research Areas

From Monolithic 3D ICs to Edge-AI accelerators — we push the boundaries of hardware innovation.

🔲

Monolithic 3D IC Design Automation

Developing EDA frameworks for M3D-IC fabrication — partitioning, placement, MIV utilization, and heterogeneous integration for next-gen chips.

🤖

Edge-AI Hardware Accelerators

Designing energy-efficient hardware accelerators for machine learning at the edge, targeting embedded and biomedical systems.

⚡

Power Delivery & Magnetics

Innovative on-chip power delivery solutions using magnetic-core inductors and TSV-based converters for 3D-IC power integrity.

🧬

Sensor Fusion & BioSystems

Integrating RF/ML-based sensors with hardware for cancer detection, agriculture monitoring, and real-time point-of-care diagnostics.

🧠

Neuromorphic Computing

Exploring energy-efficient neuromorphic architectures using emerging device technologies for AI workloads.

📐

VLSI Design & Verification

CAD innovation for standard cell design, SRAM optimization, and manufacturing-aware physical design in advanced process nodes.
