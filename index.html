Welcome

Welcome to BISON CAD, the premier lab for Integrated Circuit (IC) Design Automation, Monolithic 3D ICs, and Hardware for Machine Learning at North Dakota State University. Our mission is to train the next generation of engineers to design the future of Edge-AI and Biomedical Systems.

Interested Students: Join us to work on real-world CAD tools, advanced power delivery systems, and AI accelerators. Be part of the innovation in computer engineering hardware.
